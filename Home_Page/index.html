ARTIFACTORY

Artifactory is a blockchain-based platform that empowers Indian artisans by eliminating middlemen and providing them with a direct way to buy, sell, and mint NFTs of their traditional handcrafted masterpieces. By leveraging blockchain technology, we ensure secure transactions and transparent ownership. This innovative approach allows artisans to generate passive income while reaching a global audience, fostering financial independence. Artifactory is dedicated to preserving the rich cultural heritage of Indian art, providing fair compensation to poor artisans, and enabling them to thrive in the digital economy.

Understanding NFTs and Blockchain: A Journey Simplified

  • Understanding NFTs and Blockchain: A Journey Simplified

    An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital certificate that proves ownership of something, like an artwork, on the internet. Think of it like a digital signature that says, "This is the original item, and you own it."

  • How NFTs Work: The Digital Ownership Explained

    NFTs are created using blockchain technology, which is a secure online ledger that records all transactions. When you create an NFT, it’s like putting your artwork on a digital page that everyone can see, but only you can prove you own it.

  • The Role of Blockchain: Ensuring Security and Transparency

    Blockchain ensures that the information about who owns what is public, secure, and cannot be changed. It's like a digital book that keeps track of who has bought and sold your artwork, making sure no one can fake ownership.

  • Earning from NFTs: How Artisans Can Profit

    When you sell your artwork as an NFT, you can get paid in digital currency. Plus, if someone resells it, you can earn a small percentage each time it changes hands. This allows you to keep making money from your art even after the initial sale.
